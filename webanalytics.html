Web Analytics

Web analytics is the measurement, collection, analysis and reporting of web data for purposes of understanding and optimizing web usage:

Web analytics helps businesses understand visitors’ behavior on their websites and helps answer fundamental questions such as:
Where did visitors to my website come from?
What are visitors doing on my website?
Why did visitors complete/not complete the desired goals on my website?
The where and what are the questions that can be answered with the help of web analytics. The why is answered with the help of surveys or other voice of customers tools.

Improved Conversion Rates

CACHE web analytics team helps companies identify what visitors are doing on their websites and, based on the findings, advises how to improve conversion rates on the website. Common things we analyze include landing page performance, navigation details or bottlenecks in the registration process.
